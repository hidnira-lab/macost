<objective>
Deliver VIS-01: goal progress visualized as a pixel-art plant that grows through 5 fixed states (0/25/50/75/100%), rendered on both the goal detail page and the goal list/card view.

Purpose: Gives users an at-a-glance emotional/visual read on goal progress beyond the numeric percentage, per the PRD's polish requirement for the Expo demo.

This plan has zero dependency on the offline-sync work in 04-01/04-03 — different files, different subsystem (pure frontend, static assets, no backend/IndexedDB involvement) — runs fully in parallel with 04-01.
</objective>
